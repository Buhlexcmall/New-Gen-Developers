# Product Vision

## Problem Statement
Busy university students struggle to manage their academic responsibilities, including class schedules, assignments, and study time. This often leads to poor organization, missed deadlines, and reduced academic performance.

## Target Users
- University and college students  
- Students balancing multiple courses and responsibilities  

## Unique Value Proposition
An all-in-one productivity platform that enables students to organize schedules, track assignments, plan study sessions, centralized system.

## Key Features
- Class Schedule Management  
- Assignment Tracking and Deadline Management  
- Study Planning and Time Management  
- Notifications and Reminders  

## Success Metrics
- Number of active student users  
- Task and assignment completion rates  
- User engagement (daily and weekly usage)  
- Reduction in missed deadlines  

## Product Vision Statement (Moore’s Vision Template)

**For** busy university students  
**who** need help managing their academic responsibilities and time,  
**the** student productivity app  
**is a** mobile and web-based software solution  
**that** organizes class schedules, assignments, study plans. 

**Unlike** traditional planners or disconnected tools,  
**our product** provides an integrated, easy-to-use platform  
**that** improves productivity and academic performance.# Product Vision
