# Project name

University students often juggle lectures, assignments, tutoring sessions and other and non academic commitments
like group works and student leadershio roles.
## Target users
### University students
- Students in academic leadership roles
- Student managing multiple modules and assigmnents
- Students with bussinesses while studying
### Secondary users
- Academic mentors and advisors
## Unique Value Proposition
SmartStudy helps students stay organized, focused, and academically productive 
without distractions.

Smart academic scheduler
Task and deadline tracker
Smart reminders and Notification
Progress Tracker
Daily Focus VIew/Dashboard
