\# Wireframes



\## Overview

This section presents the UI wireframes of the Student Productivity App designed using Figma. The design focuses on helping students manage tasks, schedules, and study activities efficiently.



\## Home

!\[Home](../images/home.png)



The home screen serves as the entry point of the application, providing quick access to key features and an overview of important academic activities.



\## Study

!\[Study](../images/study.png)



This screen allows students to plan and manage their study sessions, helping them allocate time effectively and stay consistent with their learning.



\## Task

!\[Task](../images/task.png)



The task screen enables students to track assignments, deadlines, and academic responsibilities, ensuring that no important work is missed.



\## Schedule

!\[Schedule](../images/schedule.png)



This screen provides a structured view of class schedules and planned activities, helping students organize their daily and weekly routines.



\## Notifications

!\[Notifications](../images/notifications.png)



This screen provides reminders for upcoming deadlines, study sessions, and important academic events, helping students stay informed and avoid missed tasks.
