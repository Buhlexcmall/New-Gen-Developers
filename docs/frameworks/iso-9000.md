\# ISO 9000 Quality Management System



\## Overview

ISO 9000 is a set of international standards focused on quality management systems (QMS). It ensures that development processes are well-defined, consistent, and continuously improved to deliver high-quality software.



\## Key Principles



\- Customer Focus  

\- Leadership  

\- Engagement of People  

\- Process Approach  

\- Continuous Improvement  

\- Evidence-Based Decision Making  

\- Relationship Management  



\## Application to the Student Productivity App



\- Customer Focus: The system is designed around student needs  

\- Leadership: Clear goals and feature prioritization are defined  

\- Engagement: Team members collaborate and contribute feedback  

\- Process Approach: Agile methodology is followed  

\- Continuous Improvement: Regular updates based on user feedback  

\- Evidence-Based Decisions: Decisions based on usage metrics  

\- Relationship Management: Collaboration with stakeholders and users
