# User Personas and Scenarios

---

## Persona 1: Zanele (Full-Time Student)

- **Age:** 20
- **Occupation:** Second-year BCom student at a university
- **Tech Comfort:** High — uses her smartphone daily for social media and studying
- **Goal:** Stay on top of five different modules without missing any deadlines or lectures
- **Pain Points:** Relies on paper notes and WhatsApp reminders which she frequently misses; struggles to plan study time effectively before tests

### Scenario
It is Monday morning and Zanele has three assignments due this week across different subjects. She opens the Student Productivity App and checks her dashboard, which shows all three deadlines ranked by urgency. She clicks on the first assignment, marks it as "In Progress," and then opens the Study Planner to block out two hours for studying that afternoon. The app sends her a reminder at 3:00 PM, prompting her to start her session. By Friday, all three assignments are marked as "Done" and she has not missed a single deadline.


## Persona 2: Sipho (Part-Time Student with a Job)

- **Age:** 24
- **Occupation:** Part-time IT student who also works 20 hours a week at a KFC store in ultra city
- **Tech Comfort:** Medium — comfortable with apps but prefers simple, no-fuss interfaces
- **Goal:** Balance his work schedule with his studies without letting either suffer
- **Pain Points:** Forgets about assignments when he is busy at work; has no system for tracking which classes he attended or missed

### Scenario
Sipho works Tuesday and Thursday evenings, which sometimes clash with his study time. He uses the Class Schedule screen to view his weekly timetable and identifies that he has a tutorial on Wednesday afternoon he nearly forgot about. He adds a new assignment for his Networks module due in five days, sets it to "High Priority," and enables a two-day reminder notification. While at work on Thursday, his phone buzzes with a reminder about the assignment. He opens the app during his break, updates the status to "In Progress," and plans a study session for Friday morning using the Study Planner.
